The purpose of this project was to pull weather data from the OpenWeather API in order to generate plots that show how weather patterns change as proximity to the equator increases. Four factors were examined: Wind Speed, Temperature, Cloudiness, and Humidity. This shows the comparison of latitude versus wind speed. As the visualizations show, wind speeds remain fairly constant as you move closer to the equator, showing a slight icnrease as one moves away from the equator.

This visualization shows a slight increase in wind speeds as one gets farther away from the equator.
